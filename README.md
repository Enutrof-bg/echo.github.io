# Echo — Support, Assistance & Privacy Policy

Thank you for using **Echo**, your voice-driven semantic journal. We designed this application to blend as seamlessly and respectfully as possible with your cultural journey and your privacy.

If you encounter an issue, have a question, or would like to suggest an improvement, please find all the necessary information below.

---

## 🛠️ Support & FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

### 1. Are my personal data and memos secure?
**Yes, absolutely.** Echo relies on an entirely local architecture (*On-Device*). Your recordings, texts, and emotional analyses are stored directly within your iPhone's secure memory (*SwiftData*). Nothing is ever sent to third-party servers.

### 2. Does the application work without an internet connection?
Yes. Transcription, semantic search, and the Apple Intelligence model all run locally on your device. You can record your thoughts in airplane mode or right after walking out of a basement cinema without any issues.

## ⚖️ Privacy Policy (Legal Information)

**Last updated:** July 2026

At **Echo**, we firmly believe that your emotions, thoughts, and memories belong exclusively to you. That is why our application was built from the ground up around a core principle: **absolute respect for your privacy through a fully local architecture**.

### 1. Data Collection and Storage
The Echo application does not use any external servers.
* **Your inputs:** All your voice memos, text transcriptions, and generated emotional analyses remain confined within your iPhone's secure memory (*SwiftData*).
* **Biometrics / History:** No data regarding your identity, location, or cultural history is collected, sold, or shared with third-party companies.

### 2. Artificial Intelligence Processing
Echo utilizes **Apple Intelligence** features and local Machine Learning models (*On-Device*).
* All semantic processing, text vectorization, and memory summary generation are performed directly by your iPhone's processor.
* Your private data is never sent to the cloud to be processed by third-party AI services.

### 3. Analytics and Cookies
We do not use any trackers, behavioral analytics tools, or advertisements within the app. Your usage of the application remains completely anonymous.

### 4. Changes to This Policy
We reserve the right to update this privacy policy if the technical features of the app evolve. However, our core philosophy will remain unchanged: your data stays on your device.
